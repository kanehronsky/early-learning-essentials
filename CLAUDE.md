# Early Learning Essentials

A website for an early learning brand based in Perth, WA.

## Current goal
A landing page only. Clean, warm, professional. Target audience is 
parents and educators of young children.

## Design preferences
Warm colours (soft yellows, greens). Friendly but professional tone.
No dark themes. Mobile-first design — many parents browse on their phones.

## Future plans
Will eventually become an e-commerce site selling physical early learning 
products and a custom AAC app for children with communication disabilities.

## Rules
- Always write clean, well-commented code
- Ask before making large structural changes
- Keep the codebase simple — I am learning as I build
- Explain what you are doing and why when making significant changes
